Upcoming teen camp | hawaii

Come join our Teen hawaii & Adventure Camp for the two week surf experience of a lifetime! This adventure is for beginner to advanced surfers ages 14-18 who want to learn how to surf or improve their skills while being immersed in the culture of Hawaii. Come have the time of your life while surfing several of Hawii best breaks and exploring the Pahoa region hawaii style!

We have been offering camps here since 2005 as the tropical climate, year-round waves, and 80 degree water temperatures make it a go-to destination for all levels of surfers.

Teen Travel FAQs

All participants are responsible for scheduling their flights to arrive and depart from the designated airport at the appointed time.

What is the nearest airport and do you have a shuttle service?

The nearest airport is Hilo international airport.
We do have a shuttle service from and to the camps, both at the start and at the end of the camp period.

I’m nervous about traveling to Hawaii alone. Can I meet up with your instructor at the airport?

Yes, we do fix an arrangment to let on of our instructor come and pick you up.

What if my luggage doesn’t make it?

If your luggage doesn't make it we kan help you out provided gear and clothes until the problem is solved at the airport.
